About
GP ADITYAPUR

Government Polytechnic Adityapur (GPA), Jamshedpur, Jharkhand was established in the year of 1980. It is one among the 17 polytechnic colleges in the State of Jharkhand. It offers diploma courses in various Engineering branches. Admissions to this reputed college is strictly based on the entrance conducted by Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB)..

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to submit original documents for admission?

Yes, the provisional admission process takes place once the candidate brings along all the original documents required for admission and approval, pay the necessary fee and complete all the necessary formalities.

What are the office timings?

Monday - Saturday (10am to 5pm)

Hostel Accommodation?

Yes, Only for Second & Third Year Boys. Capacity : 100

Is the transport facility (College Bus) available?

No.

How can one deposit college fees?

Through Online Payment. Visa and Master Debit Card not accepted.

Is there any provision for scholarship?

Scholarship provided by the State Government and AICTE. For more details contact respective departments.
